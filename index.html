METHODOLOGY

There are teachers who, out of conviction or routine, decide to give their students a single source of learning. But we cannot ignore that human beings learn using different sources of information, which is why we cannot use a single resource, be computer, a film, a transcription, a textbook or any other: the variety of resources lies the key to success. The motivation in learning is essential, since this is the engine that moves students, activates dynamism, makes a favorable relationship connect to carry out an activity in an essential way.

SPECIFIC OBJECTIVES

1. Strengthen students' choice and self-regulation skills through the pedagogical menu.
2. Practice the English vocabulary used in relation to animals.

GENERAL OBJECTIVE

Promote teaching - learning environments that stimulate the senses and complement the self-learning process of the primary grade students of the Luis Vargas Tejada IED school using technological tools.
